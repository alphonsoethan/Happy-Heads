Summary Of The Evaluation

Anxiety

Fear of the future, panic attacks are all an indication of anxiety which is not good.

This is caused by extreme levels of stress taken or by some kind of triggers in your mind.

It is advised that you take professional counseling from a psychatrist

Social Anxiety

Social Anxiety is fear of facing people in this you are scared of face-to-face communication with other people.

This is generally caused by some sort of trauma in the past. So it is important to forget the painful past.

It is advised that you take professional counseling from a psychatrist

Insomnia/Hypersomnia

Insomnia is lack of sleep, Hypersomnia is the inability to stay awake and alert during the day.

Insomnia is caused by keeping your mind active during the night. Hypersomnia is caused by consumption of alcohol.

It is advised that you take professional counseling from a psychatrist
